LAB 5 DATA DICTIONARY
=====================

1. patient_feature_transformations.csv
--------------------------------------
Target column: risk_group

Columns:
- patient_id: Unique identifier for each synthetic patient.
- age_years: Patient age in years.
- biological_sex: Synthetic categorical variable with values Female or Male.
- height_cm: Height in centimetres.
- weight_kg: Weight in kilograms.
- bmi: Body mass index calculated from height and weight.
- systolic_bp: Synthetic systolic blood pressure measurement.
- diastolic_bp: Synthetic diastolic blood pressure measurement.
- fasting_glucose_mg_dl: Synthetic fasting glucose measurement.
- crp_mg_l: Synthetic positively skewed inflammation-related measurement.
- cholesterol_mmol_l: Synthetic cholesterol measurement.
- activity_level: Ordinal category with values Low, Moderate, and High.
- smoking_status: Nominal category with values Never, Former, and Current.
- clinic_region: Nominal category showing the clinic region.
- risk_group: Synthetic target category with values Lower, Moderate, and Higher.

2. gene_expression_pca.csv
--------------------------
Target column: diagnosis_group

Columns:
- sample_id: Unique identifier for each synthetic biological sample.
- Gene_01 to Gene_40: Synthetic gene-expression measurements.
- diagnosis_group: Synthetic class label with values Control, Inflammatory,
  and Metabolic.
